About NASA APOD

The Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) is a website provided by NASA and Michigan Technological University. Each day a different image or photograph of our universe is featured, along with a brief explanation written by a professional astronomer.

This application uses the APOD API to bring you these amazing space images directly from NASA's archives.

How It Works

Select a date to view the Astronomy Picture of the Day for that specific day, or click "Get Image" to view today's image.

Some days feature images while others feature videos, all with detailed explanations from NASA astronomers.
